Coconut Chocolate Rice Krispie Treats

I made these coconut chocolate Rice Krispies Treats to change up the normal every day recipe a little bit one day when my 6 year old son and I were looking through the kitchen to see what we could add.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 (10 ounce) package large marshmallows
  • 5 cups chocolate flavored crispy rice cereal
  • ⅓ cup sweetened flaked coconut

Steps

  1. Grease a 9x13 inch pan. Melt butter in large sauce pan over medium-low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and smooth. Remove from heat.
  2. Quickly stir the marshmallow mixture into the cereal and coconut to coat. Press into the prepared pan with the back of a buttered spoon. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
